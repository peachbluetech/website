How it works

From raw ad data to creative intelligence

peachblue automates the entire workflow. From syncing your ad accounts to delivering AI-powered analysis on every creative.

1

Connect

Securely connect your ad accounts via OAuth. peachblue syncs all your creative assets and performance data automatically, every day.

2

Analyze

AI vision models process every image and video, generating structured intelligence tags. Hook style, emotional tone, color palette, CTA type, pacing, and more.

3

Act

Explore your Creative Library, filter by any tag or metric, and use AI-powered insights to understand what's working and brief your next campaign.

Multi-platform sync

Connect Meta Ads (live), Google Ads, Amazon DSP, and TikTok. peachblue pulls creative assets and performance metrics via secure API integrations.

AI creative analysis

Every creative gets a full AI breakdown: visual design, copy strategy, hook effectiveness, and strategic recommendations. Mapped against real performance data.

Creative Library

An intelligence grid where you can filter, sort, and explore all your creatives by any tag or performance metric. Deep-dive panels show the full analysis alongside performance data.
